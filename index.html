ABOUT ME

A FEW WORDS ABOUT
MYSELF AND THINGS I LIKE TO DO

After high school I enrolled Graphic Engineering and Design studies where I gained solid knowledge about digital and print design. When I moved to Norway I had an idea that I could combine that knowledge with programming and that's how I decided to study Front end Development at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media in Oslo. I am approaching the end of my studies and I'm looking forward to impoving and expanding my knowledge in both programming and design field. I am excited about building my career as a developer and designer and I'm looking forward to new challenges and opportunities.
